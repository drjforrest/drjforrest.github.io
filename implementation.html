Implementation Plan

A comprehensive roadmap for executing the stakeholder engagement strategy

Implementation Overview

This implementation plan provides a detailed roadmap for Fair Price Pharma to effectively execute the stakeholder engagement strategy over a 12-month period. The plan is structured into four strategic phases, with clear timelines, resource requirements, and success metrics for each phase.

The implementation approach is designed to be:

  • Strategic: Focusing on high-priority stakeholders first while building capacity for broader engagement
  • Iterative: Incorporating feedback loops to continuously refine and improve engagement strategies
  • Measurable: Establishing clear metrics to track progress and effectiveness
  • Sustainable: Building internal capacity for ongoing stakeholder engagement beyond the initial implementation period

Implementation Timeline

The implementation timeline is structured into four strategic phases over a 12-month period:

Phase 1: Months 1-2

Initial Engagement

  • Finalize stakeholder mapping with internal team input
  • Develop core messaging for each stakeholder group
  • Begin outreach to highest priority stakeholders
  • Deploy Airtable tracking system and train staff
  • Launch website redesign with updated terminology
Phase 2: Months 3-4

Relationship Building

  • Launch media strategy with initial content release
  • Establish regular communication channels with key stakeholders
  • Conduct stakeholder feedback sessions to refine approach
  • Implement initial content strategy for digital channels
  • Develop MOU templates for key partnerships
