Stakeholder Engagement Strategy

This interactive matrix outlines Fair Price Pharma's full stakeholder ecosystem, including tailored objectives, actions, and engagement strategies for each group. The matrix reflects the strategic approach to managing relationships with all key stakeholders and will be maintained as a living document as the engagement strategy evolves.

Key features of the stakeholder engagement matrix include:

  • Tailored Objectives: Specific goals for each stakeholder relationship that align with Fair Price Pharma's mission
  • Actionable Strategies: Concrete steps for engaging each stakeholder group effectively
  • Influence & Interest Mapping: Assessment of each stakeholder's level of influence and interest to prioritize engagement efforts
  • Engagement Timeline: Recommended timing and frequency of interactions with each stakeholder group
  • Success Metrics: Measurable indicators to evaluate the effectiveness of engagement efforts

Interactive Stakeholder Tracking System

We've developed a comprehensive Airtable-based stakeholder management system that provides real-time tracking of stakeholder engagement, sentiment analysis, news monitoring, social media activity, and MOU status. This powerful tool enables Fair Price Pharma to:

Track Engagement Status

Monitor all interactions with stakeholders, schedule follow-ups, and ensure no relationship falls through the cracks.

Analyze Sentiment Trends

Track evolving attitudes and opinions among stakeholder groups to proactively address concerns or leverage support.

Monitor News & Social

Stay informed about relevant coverage and social media activity that could impact stakeholder relationships.

Track MOU Progress

Monitor the status of Memorandums of Understanding with key partners, from draft to signature to implementation.

Note: This is a live, interactive tracking system that will be continuously updated throughout the stakeholder engagement process. The Airtable backend can be customized to meet Fair Price Pharma's evolving needs.
