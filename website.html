Terminology Refinement

A key component of the website redesign is the strategic update of terminology to better align with current government and healthcare priorities. We've carefully reviewed all content and replaced potentially problematic language with more broadly accepted terminology.

Key Terminology Changes

safe supply → prescribed pharmaceutical alternatives

harm reduction → health and safety measures

substance users → patients with substance use disorder

addicts → individuals with substance use disorder

These terminology updates are critical for securing government support and aligning with current healthcare policy language, while still effectively communicating Fair Price Pharma's mission.
